Marisa Whitman, M.S. CCC-SLP

Clinical Director & Lead Therapist

Marisa created Funshine Speech Therapy to meet the growing need of struggling communicators in San Diego. She earned her Bachelor's and Master's degrees from California State University Northridge and holds the Certificate of Clinical Competence (CCC) from ASHA, a California speech pathology license, and a Rehabilitative Service Credential.

With over 20 years of experience, Marisa brings depth and warmth to her practice. She believes in a child- and family-centered approach, individualizing every treatment plan to the child's unique needs — and always inviting families to be active participants.

Her caseload has included children with Autism Spectrum Disorder, Fragile X, developmental delay, apraxia, fluency disorder, non-verbal learning disorder, seizure disorder, intellectual disability, ADD, dyslexia, hearing impairment, mood disorders, and learning disability.

Specialties & certifications

  • Hanen Certified — It Takes Two to Talk® parent workshop program
  • Social Thinking® — Michelle Garcia Winner curriculum
  • Kaufman Therapy Program
  • PECS (Picture Exchange Communication System)
  • Floortime and child-centered methods
  • Behavior intervention (C.A.R.D. — Center for Autism and Related Disorders)
  • Reading with TLC Program — Certified

Elizabeth Stiles-Beirne, M.S. CCC-SLP

Speech-Language Pathologist

Elizabeth brings compassion and clinical skill to the Funshine team. Holding her Master's degree and the Certificate of Clinical Competence from ASHA, she works alongside children across a range of communication needs.

Elizabeth shares Funshine's belief that therapy is most effective when it's playful, purposeful, and rooted in each child's individual strengths and goals. She works collaboratively with families to carry progress beyond the therapy session and into everyday life.

Areas of focus

  • Articulation and phonological disorders
  • Language development and delays
  • Social communication skills
  • School-age speech and language support
