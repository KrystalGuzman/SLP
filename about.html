Our Services

Who can we help?

Funshine Speech Therapy provides individualized speech services to children, young adults, and small groups in the setting most convenient for your family.

We work with children who have:

  • Articulation / phonological disorders
  • Apraxia of speech
  • Developmental / speech delay
  • Expressive / receptive language disorders
  • Fluency disorders (stuttering)
  • Social thinking / pragmatic challenges
  • Communication challenges related to autism
  • Poor speech intelligibility
  • Slow progress in school-provided speech therapy

We also provide:

  • Hanen It Takes Two to Talk® parent workshops
  • Social skills groups
  • Preschool and school-age speech screenings
  • Comprehensive speech assessment
  • Comprehensive language assessment
  • Collaborative / consultant services for IEPs
  • Flexible therapy environments (home, school, community)
  • 1, 2, 3… Connect with ME parent program

Our Approach

Child and family centered — always

01

Individualized Plans

Every child is unique. We tailor therapy to each child's specific communication needs, learning style, and family priorities — no cookie-cutter programs.

02

Family Involvement

Parents and caregivers are critical partners. We actively involve families in goal-setting and help you carry therapy strategies into everyday life.

03

Natural Environments

Therapy happens where children live and grow — at home, in school, or out in the community. Learning in real settings means real progress.
