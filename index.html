Is This Right for Us?

Does my child need speech therapy?

Children who can benefit from speech therapy may experience a variety of communication challenges. Early support makes a lasting difference.

🧠

Apraxia

Motor-speech disorder affecting the ability to plan and sequence speech movements.

🌈

Autism Spectrum

Social language and communication challenges associated with ASD and related diagnoses.

🔤

Articulation & Phonology

Specific sounds or patterns of sounds in error, affecting speech clarity.

⏱️

Developmental Delay

Late talkers and children who are hard to understand for their age.

🌊

Stuttering

Fluency disorders that interrupt the natural flow and rhythm of speech.

🏫

Slow School Progress

Children receiving school-based therapy but not making enough meaningful progress.

If you have concerns about your child's speech and language, Funshine is here for you.
