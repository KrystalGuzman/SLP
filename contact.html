What We Offer

We can help with…

🔍

Free Speech Screenings

Quick, friendly screenings for preschool and school-age children to identify whether further evaluation may be helpful.

📋

Comprehensive Assessments

In-depth speech and language evaluations to understand your child's strengths and identify areas for support.

🗣️

Individual Therapy

Personalized sessions delivered in your home, at school, or in the community — wherever works best for your family.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Workshops

Hanen "It Takes Two to Talk®" workshops and the "1, 2, 3… Connect with ME" program — for families ready to play an active role.

📍

Service Area

San Diego and surrounding communities. In-home, school, and community-based services available.

💛 Looking for the parent program?

The "1, 2, 3… Connect with ME" program and workshops are managed through Real Mom SLP.
